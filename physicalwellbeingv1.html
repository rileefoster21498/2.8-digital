Margherita pizza is a classic Italian dish that is beloved by many. It consists of a crispy, thin crust topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. The simplicity of the ingredients is what makes this pizza stand out. The tomato sauce contributes a sweet, tangy flavor that perfectly complements the creamy mozzarella cheese. The basil leaves add freshness and a hint of herbal aroma.
