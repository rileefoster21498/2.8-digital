Margherita Pizza Recipe

You will need:












Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 240C.
  2. Defrost and prepare your cauliflower pizza base for use. Place your two pizza bases onto oven safe trays that have been lined with baking paper.
  3. Now it is time to make the sauce. Heat a medium sized pan over medium heat with a drizzle of olive oil. If you are choosing to use garlic and/or onion, cook these in the pan until softened or for five minutes.
  4. After your onions and garlic are softened, place your can of tinned tomatoes and tomato paste into the pan, stirring until mixed. If you would like a sweeter sauce, add one teaspoon of sugar into your sauce and stir until you can no longer see the grains of sugar. Keep your sauce over a medium heat for 8 minutes, or until your sauce thickens to your likening.
  5. After your sauce is done, divide it evenly and spread it over your two cauliflower pizza bases. Sprinkle your mozzarella cheese on top of your bases and sauce until you have a generous coverage of cheese. To top the pizza, add basil leaves, italian herbs or any other herb or spice of your choosing.
  6. After your pizzas are topped, place your pizzas in the oven for 8-10 minutes or until they are golden and crispy.
  7. Slice your pizza and enjoy!

    8. Pro Tip: for a more traditional taste, top your pizza with cherry tomatoes to enhance the flavour of your pizza.
